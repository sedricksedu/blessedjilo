Meet Our Team

Our Leadership Team Dedicated to Community Transformation

Michael James Ebong

CEO/Founder

Fr. Ebong has vast experience in working with communities to achieve goals and support vulnerable members. Passionate about orphans, youth, women, and the elderly, he promotes livelihood and sustainable development. He is also a priest and currently pursuing higher studies in Rome. Fr. Ebong works as the CEO, providing leadership and management support to the team.

Fr. Geoffrey Onimo Aporoson

Catholic Priest

Fr. Geoffrey Onimo Aporoson is a priest of the Diocese of Lira and patron of Blessed Jildo and Daudi Livelihood Transformation Initiative. He is instrumental in championing development and the well-being of infants, shaping the organization’s destiny through his distinctive guidance.

Denis Acobi Cosmas

Programmes Manager

Denis Acobi Cosmas leads and coordinates community-driven projects, ensuring alignment with BJDLTI’s mission of social change. With over 8 years of experience in agriculture, food security, and sustainable development, he brings expertise in climate resilience, agri-enterprise models, and policy advocacy.
