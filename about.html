Who We Are

Blessed Jildo and Daudi Livelihood Transformation Initiative is a transformative organization dedicated to empowering communities in Uganda through innovative agricultural practices, skills development, and self-sustainability programs. We work towards improving food security, creating resilient livelihoods, and fostering sustainable development, with a specific focus on vulnerable populations such as women, youth, and rural farmers.

Our mission is to enable communities to overcome poverty and build sustainable futures by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to thrive. Through our programs, we aim to address critical issues such as food insecurity, lack of education, and limited access to resources, all while promoting environmental sustainability and community-driven development.

Learn More About Our Impact

Innovative Agriculture

We focus on innovative agricultural practices that empower farmers to enhance productivity, improve food security, and create sustainable livelihoods. Our programs introduce modern farming techniques, sustainable crop management, and environmental conservation practices to improve long-term resilience.

Skills Development

We offer skills training programs in agriculture, vocational skills, and entrepreneurship to enhance self-sufficiency and open doors for economic opportunities. By empowering individuals with valuable skills, we help them create sustainable livelihoods and improve their quality of life.

Community Empowerment

Our approach places communities at the heart of development. We work closely with local leaders and vulnerable groups, particularly women and youth, to identify challenges and co-create sustainable solutions that empower them to lead and drive change in their own communities.

Our Vision

Building Resilient, Self-Sustaining Communities

To build resilient and self-sustaining communities that can thrive independently and contribute meaningfully to national development. Through our holistic approach, we aim to empower individuals with skills, resources, and innovative solutions to enhance livelihoods and ensure a brighter, more sustainable future for all.

Our Mission

To enhance the livelihoods of vulnerable communities through sustainable agriculture, innovative skills development, health interventions, and climate-smart solutions.

Core Values

  • Community Focus: Putting community needs at the center of our work.
  • Sustainability: Advocating for long-term, eco-friendly solutions.
  • Innovation: Promoting inventive approaches to community challenges.
  • Empowerment: Building the capacity of individuals to achieve self-reliance.
